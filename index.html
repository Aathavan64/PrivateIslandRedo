Welcome to Phester Rock

Crime capital of the world

Phester Rock - an island nation in the middle of the ocean. It’s long been used as a harbour for travelling cargo ships, and a stopover for international flights.

In the past Phester Rock (also known as Phester’s or The Rock island residents) has even been a popular tourist destination, due to its diverse climates and land regions in such a short geographical distance. The island is a weather anomaly!

Unfortunately, that tourism and economic boost to the island nation has dried up in recent years as rampant corruption at the political level has trickled down into civil unrest and increased crime rates in the daily lives of Phester Rockians.

That’s where you come in. We need you to join our team of Detectives as a hired outsider who can come in and help us clean up this Island.
